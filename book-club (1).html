About the Book Club

Welcome to the Conrad Book Club! This group is designed for hospitality professionals and self-help enthusiasts who are passionate about personal growth and fostering a culture of excellence. Each month, we’ll gather for a 1-hour meeting, hosted by a rotating facilitator, to discuss insights from a curated list of books. Join us to exchange ideas, gain inspiration, and strengthen your commitment to creating exceptional experiences.

Reading List by Month

  • January: Be My Guest by Conrad Hilton
    Discover the inspiring story of Conrad Hilton’s journey to building one of the world's most renowned hospitality brands.
  • February: The Power of Habit by Charles Duhigg
    Learn how habits shape our lives and how to harness their power for personal and professional success.
  • March: Setting the Table by Danny Meyer
    Explore the principles of hospitality and leadership from one of the most successful restaurateurs.
  • April: Start with Why by Simon Sinek
    Uncover the importance of finding your “why” and how it drives leadership and innovation.
  • May: Delivering Happiness by Tony Hsieh
    Dive into the journey of Zappos’ CEO and his philosophy of creating a company culture centered on happiness.
  • June: Unreasonable Hospitality by Will Guidara
    Discover how exceptional service can transform ordinary moments into extraordinary experiences.
  • July: How to Win Friends and Influence People by Dale Carnegie
    Timeless advice on building meaningful relationships and influencing people effectively.
  • August: Atomic Habits by James Clear
    Learn how small changes lead to remarkable results in both life and work.
  • September: Emotional Intelligence by Daniel Goleman
    Understand how emotional intelligence impacts success and learn to improve it.
  • October: Ego is the Enemy by Ryan Holiday
    Explore the dangers of ego and strategies to overcome it in pursuit of success.
